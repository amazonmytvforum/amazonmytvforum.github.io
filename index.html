🔥 Amazon Fire TV Stick — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Fire Stick is made by Amazon, so it links automatically during setup. Use these steps to re-link or activate on a new account.

1
On Fire Stick home screen, go to Settings → My Account.
2
Select Amazon Account. If already registered, click Deregister first.
3
Open Amazon Prime Video app. A code will appear.
4
On your phone, go to amazon.com/mytv and sign in.
5
Enter the code from your Fire Stick screen and click Register Device. Done!

📺 Roku — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Activate Amazon Prime Video on any Roku device using amazon.com/mytv.

1
On Roku home screen, go to Streaming Channels → Search Channels.
2
Search for Amazon Video and install the channel.
3
Open Amazon Video and select Sign In. A 6-digit code appears.
4
Visit amazon.com/mytv on your phone and sign into your Amazon account.
5
Enter the Roku code and click Register Device. Your Roku is now activated.

🔵 Samsung Smart TV — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Works on all Samsung Smart TVs from 2016 onwards with Tizen OS.

1
Press the Home button on your Samsung remote to open Smart Hub.
2
Navigate to the Amazon Prime Video app and launch it.
3
Select "Register on the Amazon Website" — a 6-digit code appears.
4
Open amazon.com/mytv on your phone and log in.
5
Type the 6-digit code shown on your Samsung TV and click Register Device.

🔴 LG Smart TV — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Works on LG Smart TVs with WebOS (2014 and later models).

1
Press Home on your LG remote to access the launcher bar.
2
Find and open the Amazon Prime Video app (install from LG Content Store if needed).
3
Choose Sign In → Register on the Amazon Website. Note your 6-digit code.
4
Go to amazon.com/mytv on your phone and sign in to Amazon.
5
Enter the 6-digit code and click Register Device. Your LG TV is now connected.

🍎 Apple TV — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Works on Apple TV HD (4th gen) and Apple TV 4K.

1
On your Apple TV, download Amazon Prime Video from the App Store.
2
Open the app and select Sign In. A code will appear on your TV screen.
3
On your iPhone or Mac, visit amazon.com/mytv and log into your Amazon account.
4
Enter the code shown on your Apple TV and click Register Device.
5
Your Apple TV will automatically refresh — Prime Video is ready to stream!

🎬 Sony Android TV — amazon.com/mytv Activation

Works on Sony Bravia TVs running Android TV (2015 and newer).

1
Press Home on your Sony remote and open Google Play Store.
2
Search for and install Amazon Prime Video if not already installed.
3
Open the app, choose Sign In, and get your activation code.
4
Visit amazon.com/mytv on your phone or computer.
5
Enter the code and click Register Device. Sony TV is now activated.

Universal Activation Process

Regardless of your device, amazon.com/mytv activation always follows this 4-step flow.

01

Get Code on TV

02

Open on phone/computer within 10 minutes of seeing the code.

03

04

Enter & Activate
