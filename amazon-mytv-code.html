How to Enter Code at amazon.com/mytv

Follow these steps in order. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.

1

Open Prime Video on TV

Launch the Amazon Prime Video app on your Smart TV, Fire Stick, or Roku.

2

See Your Code

A 6-digit activation code will appear automatically on your TV screen.

3

Open amazon.com/mytv

On phone or computer, open your browser and go to amazon.com/mytv.

4

Sign In to Amazon

Log in to your Amazon account (the one with your Prime subscription).

5

Type the Code

Enter your 6 digits in the box on amazon.com/mytv and click Register Device.

6

Done! ✓

Your TV will automatically refresh and Prime Video will be ready to use.

FAQ – Amazon MyTV Code

Your code appears on your TV screen automatically when you open the Amazon Prime Video app and select "Register on the Amazon Website." It is always 6 digits long.
Simply close and reopen the Prime Video app on your TV. A new 6-digit code will appear. Then go back to amazon.com/mytv and enter the new code.
The code has likely expired (they expire in ~10 minutes). Restart the Prime Video app on your TV to get a fresh code, then re-enter it at amazon.com/mytv immediately.
You need an Amazon account to activate a device at amazon.com/mytv. A Prime subscription is needed to watch Prime Video content, but you can activate the app and browse free content without it.
